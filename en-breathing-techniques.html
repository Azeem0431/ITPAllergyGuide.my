Natural Substitute

Mashed Banana

Mashed banana is an excellent egg substitute that provides moisture and dense texture in baked goods without using eggs.

Substitution Ratio

🥚

1 Egg

=

🍌

1/2 Banana

*Use approximately 1/4 cup of mashed banana for each egg.

Advantages

  • ✓ Adds moisture to cakes.
  • ✓ Natural sweetener (reduce sugar).
  • ✓ Suitable for muffins and pancakes.

Disadvantages

  • ! Leaves a strong banana flavor.
  • ! Cake texture may be slightly heavier.

💡 Tips For Best Results

Use very ripe bananas (with black spots on the skin). Very ripe bananas are easier to mash until smooth and have higher natural sugar content, making them a better binding agent.
