Peanut Allergy

Peanut allergy is the leading cause of fatal allergic reactions. This allergy is usually lifelong.

! Where Do Peanuts Hide?

Obvious Foods

  • Peanut butter
  • Trail mix / Mixed nuts
  • Peanut oil (Cold-pressed)

Hidden Sources

  • Satay sauce and peanut gravy
  • Asian cuisine (Thai, Chinese, Malay)
  • Chocolate, biscuits, and pastries

Emergency Signs

🫁

Respiratory

Airway constriction, severe shortness of breath, or persistent coughing.

👄

Drastic Swelling

Swelling of the tongue, lips, and throat making swallowing difficult.

🧠

Nervous System

Severe dizziness, feeling faint, or loss of consciousness.

❤️

Cardiac

Pulse becomes very weak or suddenly very fast.

Myth vs Fact

Famous Myth

"Smelling peanuts can cause death."

Fact: Most reactions occur through ingestion. However, airborne peanut dust can be dangerous.

Oil Myth

"Peanut oil is always dangerous."

Fact: Highly refined oil is usually safe, but cold-pressed oil is a Danger Zone.

Emergency 999 Protocol

  1. 01

    Use EpiPen Immediately

    Inject epinephrine into the outer thigh. Do not wait for severe symptoms.

  2. 02

    Call Emergency Services

    Say "Anaphylaxis" to the 999 operator. Requires 4 hours of hospital observation.
