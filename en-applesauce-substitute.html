Moistening Agent

Applesauce

Applesauce acts as an excellent moistening agent. It is very suitable for baked goods that require a soft yet dense texture like brownies or muffins.

Substitution Ratio

🥚

1 Egg

=

🍎

1/4 Cup Apple

Use the "Unsweetened" version (no added sugar).

Advantages

  • ✓ Very moist and soft texture.
  • ✓ Neutral flavor (not as strong as banana).
  • ✓ Reduces cholesterol in cooking.

Bakery Tips:

If you use applesauce, add a little baking powder (about 1/2 teaspoon) to the batter to help the cake rise, as apples tend to make the batter heavy.

How to Make Your Own:

Peel, core, and chop the apples. Boil with a little water until soft, then mash or blend until smooth.

Suitable for: Fruit Cakes, Brownies & Pancakes
