Allergy Categories

Explore various types of allergies, understand their triggers, and learn how to manage symptoms effectively through our comprehensive guides.

Most Common

Food Allergies

Immune reaction to specific food proteins like nuts, seafood, and milk.

Dermatology

Skin Allergies

Conditions like Eczema and Hives that cause itching and inflammation on the skin.

Respiratory

Respiratory Allergies

Allergies to dust, pollen or mold affecting the respiratory tract and sinuses.

Pets

Pet Allergies

Reaction to proteins in animal skin cells, saliva or fur of cats and dogs.

Ophthalmology

Eye Allergies

Also known as allergic conjunctivitis, causing red, itchy and watery eyes.

Medical

Drug Allergies

Abnormal immune system reaction to certain medications like antibiotics (penicillin).
