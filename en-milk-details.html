Most Common in Infants

Milk Allergy

Involves an immune reaction to milk proteins like Casein and Whey. Often confused with lactose intolerance.

Allergy vs Lactose Intolerance

!

Milk Allergy

Involves the immune system. The body rejects milk proteins. Can cause hives, rashes, or difficulty breathing (anaphylaxis).

i

Lactose Intolerance

Involves the digestive system. The body has difficulty digesting milk sugar. Causes bloating, gas, and diarrhea only.

02 Where Are Milk Proteins Found?

● Common Foods

  • Butter, Cheese, and Yogurt
  • Ice Cream and Pudding
  • Milk Chocolate

● Terms on Labels

  • Casein / Caseinate
  • Whey
  • Ghee
