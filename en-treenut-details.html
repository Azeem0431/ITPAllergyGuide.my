Tree Nut Allergy

Tree nuts are among the food allergens that most frequently cause severe allergic reactions and often persist for life.

Main Types of Tree Nuts

Almond

Cashew

Walnut

Pistachio

Watch Out for These Products

  • ● Nutella / Chocolate Spread (Hazelnut)
  • ● Pesto Sauce (Pine Nuts)
  • ● Marzipan (Almond Paste)
  • ● Nut oils in salads
  • ● Alternative Milks (Almond/Cashew)
  • ● Personal Care Products (Almond Oil)

🇲🇾 Note for Malaysians

Cashews are often used in kurma gravy, luxurious rendang, or as a garnish for nasi beriani. If you eat out, always inform the waiter about your allergy because many "hidden ingredients" use tree nuts to thicken the gravy.

Safety Protocol:

🚨

Avoid 'Bulk Bins'

The risk of cross-contamination is very high in self-serve sections at supermarkets.

🏥

EpiPen Is Essential

Tree nut allergies act very quickly. Always carry your adrenaline auto-injector at all times.
