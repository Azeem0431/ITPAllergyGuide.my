Anaphylaxis

The most extreme allergic reaction affecting the entire body. Can cause death within minutes if airways close or blood pressure drops sharply.

! Recognize Key Signs

🫁

Airway

Hoarse voice, shortness of breath, wheezing, or swollen tongue.

📉

Blood Pressure

Pale, feeling faint, body becomes weak or suddenly cold.

🧠

Consciousness

Confusion, agitation, or unconsciousness (fainting).

🍓

Skin

Hives spreading rapidly all over the body.

Immediate Action (FAST)

1

Call 999

Say: "I HAVE A CASE OF ANAPHYLAXIS" so an ambulance with oxygen is dispatched.

2

Inject Adrenaline (EpiPen)

Inject into outer thigh. Can go through clothing. Hold for 10 seconds.

3

Lay Down & Elevate Legs

Do not stand. If vomiting, turn body to the side (Recovery Position).

FAST

IMPORTANT: Never do this

  • ❌ Do not give food/drink oral medication.
  • ❌ Do not let the person walk.
  • ❌ Do not place a high pillow under the head.
  • ❌ Do not delay EpiPen injection.
