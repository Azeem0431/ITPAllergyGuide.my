Elimination Diet Process

01
Week 1-2: Detox

Eat only fresh and safe foods (Rice, Vegetables, Fresh Meat).

02
Week 3-4: Monitor

Observe skin improvement. If itching subsides, your system is clear.

03
Week 5-6: Challenge

Start consuming 1 allergen type again to confirm the cause of irritation.
