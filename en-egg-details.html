Egg Allergy

Occurs when the immune system reacts to proteins in egg whites or yolks. It is the second most common allergy among Malaysian children.

1 Egg White vs Egg Yolk

"The majority of individuals are allergic to proteins in egg whites, however, separating them at home makes it difficult to guarantee 100% safety."

Technical Label Terms

  • ● Albumin (Main protein)
  • ● Lysozyme (In cheese)
  • ● Meringue / Surimi
  • ● Lecithin (If egg-based)
