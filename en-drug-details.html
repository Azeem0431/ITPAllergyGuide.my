Drug Allergies:
Recognize & Prevent

A serious and unexpected immune reaction to certain medications. Learn the difference before it's too late.

DRUGS

⚖️ Allergy vs Side Effect

Side Effect

Expected and common. Example: Drowsiness after cold medicine.

Drug Allergy

Unexpected immune reaction. Example: Itchy rash, swelling or shortness of breath.

Immediate

Minutes to Hours

Anaphylaxis or hives. Occurs shortly after taking the medication.

Late

Hours to Days

Red rash or itching. Takes 2-3 days to appear.

Very Late

1 to 3 Weeks

Serious reactions like SJS. Involves fever and organ damage.

High-Risk Medications

🧫

Penicillin

Main cause of antibiotic allergy.

💊

NSAIDs (Painkillers)

Aspirin, Ibuprofen, Ponstan.

Severe Skin Reactions (SCAR)

Medical emergency if rash is accompanied by blisters and high fever. Can be life-threatening.
