Healthy Home Tips

Mite-Free Zone.

Change how you clean your home to reduce allergy attacks by up to 70%.

🛏️ Focus: Bedroom

Wash Water Temperature

Wash sheets and pillowcases with water at 60°C to completely kill dust mites.

Allergen Protectors

Use zippered anti-dust mite covers for mattresses to prevent mite breeding inside the fibers.

🚫 Don't Make These Mistakes!

🧹
Sweeping With a Regular Broom

Brooms scatter dust into the air. Use a damp mop or vacuum with a HEPA filter.

🧸
Furry Toys Left Exposed

Soft toys are dust magnets. Tip: Place them in a bag and freeze for 24 hours to kill mites.

Essential Equipment List

HEPA Vacuum

Microfiber Cloth

Air Purifier

Dehumidifier
