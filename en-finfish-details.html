Protein Allergen

Finned Fish

Finned fish allergy is usually lifelong. It is different from shellfish allergy; if you are allergic to fish, it does not necessarily mean you are allergic to shrimp.

1 Fish That Commonly Cause Allergies

Salmon

Tuna

Cod / Salted Fish

Mackerel

Main Cause: Parvalbumin

Most people are allergic to the protein parvalbumin found in fish muscle tissue. This protein is very stable and is not destroyed even when the fish is cooked or processed.

Poisoning vs Allergy

Sometimes people think they are allergic, but actually have Scombroid Poisoning (from spoiled fish), which has similar symptoms like hives.

2 Be Careful with These Products

Category Product Examples
Sauces & Seasonings Fish sauce (Nam Pla), Budu, Worcestershire Sauce, Caesar Salad Dressing.
Processed Foods Surimi (Imitation Crab), Fish Balls, Fish Crackers, Fish Gelatin.
Others Fish oil (Omega-3), cat/dog food, marine collagen supplements.
