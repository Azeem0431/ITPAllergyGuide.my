Respiratory

Understanding Respiratory Allergies

Respiratory allergies are not just a common cold. They are an immune response to microscopic particles that disrupt your quality of life and sleep.

1 Main Triggers in Malaysia

🌫️

House Dust Mites

#1 Cause in Malaysia

Thrive in pillows, mattresses, and fabric sofas with high humidity (Humidity > 60%).

🐈

Animal Dander

Cats & Dogs

It's not the fur, but the protein on dead skin (dander), saliva, and urine of animals.

🍄

Mold & Spores

Bathroom & AC Issues

Thrive on damp walls and unserviced air conditioning systems.

🪳

Cockroach Allergens

Proteins in cockroach droppings, saliva, and bodies are a major asthma trigger in urban areas.

🏭

Haze & PM2.5

Fine particles from open burning that seep into blood vessels and worsen eczema.

☀️

Sweat & Heat

Malaysia's humid weather triggers 'heat rash' and extreme itchiness.

2 Medical Treatment Options

Oral Antihistamines

Zyrtec, Clarityne, Telfast

Quick relief for itching and sneezing. Choose 'Second Generation' (Loratadine/Cetirizine) to avoid drowsiness.

Nasal Corticosteroids

Nasonex, Avamys

Long-term treatment for chronic nasal congestion by addressing the root cause of immune inflammation.

Steroid Cream / TCI

Hydrocortisone, Elomet

Used to relieve eczema redness. Use as directed by your doctor to avoid skin thinning.

💡

Important Note: Avoid overusing 'Decongestant Nasal Spray' for more than 5 consecutive days to prevent "Rebound Congestion".

Allergy Myths vs Facts

❌

MYTH: "Drug/food allergies disappear when we grow up."

FACT: Some allergies usually persist for life. New allergies can appear at any time.

✅

FACT: "Allergies can cause mental stress."

Sleep disturbances due to chronic itching can lead to anxiety and depression.

2 Medical Treatment Archive

💊

Antihistamine Generations

GEN-1 (SEDATING)

Chlorpheniramine (Piriton). Suitable for nighttime sleep.

GEN-2 (NON-SEDATING)

Loratadine, Cetirizine. Gold standard for daily use.

🧴

Topicals & Sprays

Nasal Steroid (Avamys/Nasonex)

Most effective medication for sinusitis by reducing nasal swelling.

Barrier Cream (Ceramide)

Repairs the skin barrier in eczema patients so allergens cannot easily enter.

⚠️ When Should You Go to the Hospital Immediately?

Do not wait if you experience:

  • Severe shortness of breath (wheezing)
  • Wheezing or whistling sound while breathing
  • Lips or nails turning blue
  • Sharp, stabbing chest pain
